Love Your Bear

Keep your TedBud soft and huggable for years to come.

🚿

Washing

Spot clean with mild soap and water. For a deep clean, place in a pillowcase and wash on gently cycle with cold water.

🌬️

Drying

Air dry is best. If you must use a dryer, use the 'No Heat' or 'Air Fluff' setting to protect the fur fibers.

💆

Grooming

Use a soft bristle brush or a pet slicker brush to gently fluff up the fur if it gets matted over time.
