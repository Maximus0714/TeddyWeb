Our Story

Defining the art of comfort since 2024.

Our Inspiration

We started TedBud with a simple reflection: why did the toys of our childhood feel so much more magical? We wanted to recreate that profound coziness and nostalgia. Every stitch is designed not just to be looked at, but to be fiercely loved, held tight, and kept forever.

A true companion provides a soft place to land at the end of every day.

A Bear For Every Personality

We believe that your TedBud should reflect who you are. That’s why we source the highest quality fabrics in a variety of emotional palettes. Whether you love the classic, vibrant orange, or the serene calm of sky blue—there is a bear patiently waiting to be your buddy.

Our Mission & Sustainability

To create a companion that isn't just a toy, but a piece of home. Our eco-cloud filling is made from 100% recycled materials. Every TedBud is finished by hand, ethically sourced, and entirely unique. Just like you.
